Base-G Beach

Jayapura

Base-G, which is one of Jayapura's city icons, is full of historical value. The name Base-G comes from the beach's initial function as a "homebase" for Allied troops in World War 2. The letter "G" (alphabetical order 7) indicates that this place is the 7th "homebase". It is said that at that time , Base-G Beach became the point of gathering strength for the Allied forces led by General McArthur before attacking Japanese troops in the Philippines. Compared to other beaches in Jayapura City, Base G Beach has bigger waves because of its geographic location which is directly opposite the Pacific Ocean. But there is no need to worry because the manager has installed a coral breaker about one kilometer from the beach to reduce the size of the waves. From a distance, you can see a long, sloping coastline with blue water. Base G beach looks very pretty and beautiful. Plus the green trees that grow around the beach, really are a blend of extraordinary natural beauty.

Pemancar Hill

Jayapura

Usually people come to the transmitter hill in the morning,because indeed from the top of the hill the sunrise will be seen above the sea. Meanwhile, in the afternoon at the transmitter hill, the sunset will be covered by the Cyclop ridge. But due to the consideration of the location of the basecamp in Abepura, it is rather difficult to enjoy the sunrise on this transmitter hill. The journey from Abepura to the top of the transmitter hill in Jayapura is about thirty minutes by car. It is called the transmitter hill because the transmitter for several television stations is installed on this hill. The transmitter hill is also one of the highest points in the city of Jayapura. When viewed from a distance there is a large inscription that says JAYAPURA CITY which will glow at night, next to it is a large cross marking Jayapura city.

Sentani Lake

Jayapura

Lake Sentani has very rich natural resources. There are various species of freshwater fish that live in this lake. Some of them are endemic to Sentani. Apart from presenting natural diversity, the lake in the Jayapura area also has a beautiful hill called Teletubbies Hill. Those who visit this place will not miss the beauty of the sunset, a moment that many tourists are waiting for. Tourists who visit this lake can enjoy the beauty of the hill by climbing the MacArthur Monument and seeing the green panorama of the trees from the top of the hill. The famous American World War legend, General MacArthur is said to have lived in Lake Sentani. Not only its natural charm, Lake Sentani also has other historical wealth. The Papua Archeology Center in research in the waters of the western part of Lake Sentani, Doyo Lama Village, Waibu District, Jayapura Regency, Papua, found poles of prehistoric houses several years ago. The beauty of Lake Sentani is also flanked by 22 islands scattered around the lake. Tourists can go around the lake using a rented tour boat to enjoy the exotic panorama of Lake Sentani. Tourists can also swim, fish, dive, water ski and culinary tours in the lake area. Of the 22 islands, Asei Island is one that is quite famous. There, they can buy Papuan handicrafts. Some of them are Noken, Papuan bags that have gone global, bark souvenirs with various motifs, Papuan batik, Papuan motif t-shirts, and others.

Border Post of Republic of Indonesia

Jayapura

The RI-PNG border in Skouw, Muaratami district, Jayapura City is a new tourist attraction for tourists, both local and foreign tourists. Tablanusu Beach

Jayapura

The tourist location of Tablanusu Beach is located in the Depapre district, Jayapura Regency. The residents here, in the past, their livelihoods as cultivators, now are not. Now his profession besides being a fisherman,he also works as a tour guide and tour services. On this basis, the fishing village which has an area of ​​about 230.5 hectares, was proclaimed by the Jayapura Regency government to become a tourist village. Familiarizing yourself with the villagers here is fairly easy. Because the public's welcome is warm and friendly to foreigners, it has further confirmed how special it is to have a vacation to Tablanusu Beach. Tourism here continues to improve itself. What does Tablanusu mean? Consists of two syllables. Tepara means tribe, local people. And Onusu means the setting of the sun. Tablanusu, meaning where the sun sets. Vacation to Tablanusu Beach is all natural, still beautiful. And of course, exotic. That's the impression of tourists when staying in the tourist village of Tablanusu. You can enjoy this impression when traveling from Depapre Pier by using an outboard engine boat to Tablanusu Pier. Along the way, tourists are guaranteed to be amazed.
